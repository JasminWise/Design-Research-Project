User

UX is about understanding user design and what the users need and value. It is also about making it accessible to users with disabilities. I choose to learn more about navigation design and how to make a website more accessible to all users.

Navigation design must be able to guide users and what they can and cannot click. The colors also must be easy on the eyes with users who have trouble with their eyes. Making your nav bars in a certain order and making everything line up could help users with OCD and make it a more enjoyable experience for them.
